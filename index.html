Our Solution

Four pillars that open the door to financial dignity.

Refugee Individual Number
Uses the existing RIN as the sole registration credential — no passport, no birth certificate, no barriers. Identity verified in seconds.
Digital Wallet Platform
A secure, mobile-first transaction environment accessible on any smartphone — even entry-level devices with USSD for low internet areas.
User Empowerment Tools
Savings goals, loyalty programs, referral bonuses, vouchers, and dividend payouts built directly into the app — building real economic agency.
Vendor Ecosystem
Integrated with M-Pesa, SACCOs, and partner banks — linking users to existing financial rails instantly. No new infrastructure needed.
How It Works

Three steps to financial access.

01
Register with your RIN
No documents beyond your Refugee Individual Number. Onboarding completes in under 2 minutes on any mobile device — smartphone or basic phone.
02
Access your wallet
Send, receive, and store funds securely. Works via app or USSD — even with zero internet connectivity in remote settlements and camps.
03
Grow financially
Set savings goals, earn loyalty rewards, and access the full M-Pesa and SACCO partner network to build real, lasting economic standing.
